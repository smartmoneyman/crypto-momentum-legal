Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Crypto Momentum Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: Crypto Momentum Tracker does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. We respect your privacy completely. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do NOT collect any personal information. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment or financial information
  • Account credentials or login information

1.2 Usage Data

The app does NOT collect any analytics, usage statistics, or tracking data. We do not use any third-party analytics services.

1.3 Local Storage Only

All app data, including your alerts, favorites, and preferences, are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the app

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use any information. The app functions entirely on your device without sending any data to external servers, except:

2.1 Cryptocurrency Price Data

The app fetches real-time cryptocurrency prices from Binance's public API. This connection:

  • Is made directly from your device to Binance servers
  • Does not pass through our servers
  • Does not include any identifying information about you
  • Is subject to Binance's Privacy Policy

2.2 Push Notifications

If you enable price alerts, the app uses your device's local notification system. No data is sent to external push notification services. Notifications are generated locally on your device.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data. Specifically:

  • No data is sold to advertisers
  • No data is shared with analytics companies
  • No data is provided to marketing services
  • No data is transferred to law enforcement (there's no data to transfer)

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, as we don't collect any information at all.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers, the security of your data depends on your device's security. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device operating system updated
  • Using a device lock screen
  • Being cautious about installing other apps

6. Third-Party Services

The app connects to the following external service:

6.1 Binance API

  • Purpose: Fetch real-time cryptocurrency prices
  • Data shared: None (public API requests only)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.binance.com/en/privacy

7. Your Rights and Choices

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete from our end. However, you can:

  • Clear all app data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Disable notifications in your device settings

8. International Users

The app can be used from anywhere in the world. Since we don't collect any data, there are no international data transfer concerns.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Open Source

We believe in transparency. Our app's code can be reviewed to verify our privacy claims. You can confirm that no data collection mechanisms are present.
