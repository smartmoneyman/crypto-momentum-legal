# Privacy Policy for Crypto Momentum

**Last updated:** January 04, 2026

## Introduction

Crypto Momentum ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Crypto Momentum mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

**Local Storage Data:**
- Alert configurations (symbols, thresholds, notification preferences)
- User preferences (display settings, filters)
- Favorites list (saved cryptocurrency symbols)
- Alert history (triggered alerts log)

**This data is stored ONLY on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.**

### Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Photos or media files
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Payment information

### Third-Party Services

Our app connects to the following third-party services:

**Binance API:**
- Purpose: Retrieve real-time cryptocurrency market data
- Data sent: API requests for public market data
- Data received: Market prices, volumes, open interest, funding rates
- Privacy policy: https://www.binance.com/en/privacy

We do NOT share any personal information with Binance or any third parties.

## Data Security

- All user data is stored locally on your device
- We use secure HTTPS connections for API requests
- No user data is transmitted to our servers
- No analytics or tracking services are used

## Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
